Final Project Proposal

Topic

This website is a Korean makeup guide for my younger sister so she knows what makeup products to look out for on her upcoming trip to Korea. I curated highly rated products, as well as some of my personal favorites, and will display them in gallery form across different pages on my website.

Audience

The audience is my younger sister, as well as anyone who is looking to explore Korean makeup.

Scope

The website will have 6 pages. A homepage with navigation and a suggestion form for more products, as well as 3 main pages and 2 subpages.

  • Homepage
  • Eye Page (main)
  • Eye-Eyeliner Page (sub)
  • Eye-Glitter Page (sub)
  • Face Page (main)
  • Lip Page (main)

Design & Style

I want a simple, yet cute design with y2k inspired png graphics. The page should also be very colorful and bright.
