glitter

  • glitter is one of my most absolute favorite parts about makeup
  • i like putting glitter on both my eyelids and aegyosal (eye bags)
  • i also usually prefer iridescent glitters compared to one shaded ones; iridescence glimmers & reflects other colors so i think it looks prettier
  • note: there's a difference between shimmer and glitter; shimmer is shiny, but with glitter you can see the individual glitter specks
  • other popular brands for glitter: Dasique, 3CE, Holika Holika

Rom&nd

Better Than Palette Light and Glitter Garden
  • glitter eyeshadow palette
  • there's a bit of fallout, especially when using a lot
  • fine and chunky glitters, but no super-fine

Etude

Tear Eye Liner
  • liquid eye glitter
  • super-fine shimmery glitter
  • this one is super popular; an og glitter product
  • #03 PURE SPARKLING is iridescent!

Unleashia

Glitterpedia Eye Palette
  • glitter eye palette
  • no super chunky glitters like Rom&nd
  • i would say these look more iridescent than the rom&nd palette glitters

Peripera

Sugar Twinkle Duo Eye Stick
  • glitter eyeshadow stick
  • more of a shimmer than a glitter; this one's mainly for highlighting your aegyosal
  • #01 DEWY NUDE or #03 GLIMMERING PINK
