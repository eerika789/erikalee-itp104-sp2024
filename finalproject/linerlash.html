liner & lash

  • i personally like pencil liners the most because its easy to wipe away if you mess up
  • for liquid liners, Heroine Make by KISSME is a Japanese brand popular for waterproof eyeliners and mascaras
  • if you want your eyelashes to hold a curl, you need waterproof mascara, or an asian mascara primer/fixer
  • another tip to hold an eyelash curl is by using heat; some heat up their eyelash curler with a blowdryer, Korean makeup artists burn a wooden stick and hold it against their lashes
  • * use the previous tips with caution! *
