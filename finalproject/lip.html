lip

  • out of all korean makeup products, i'd say lips are my favorite
  • not listed here, but one of my daily products is the ETUDE Dear Darling Water Tint; texture is like water and it stains your lips; follow up with vaseline/lip balm!
  • honestly you can't go wrong with any korean lip product, but I would say the biggest brands are Rom&nd and Peripera
  • i like lip glosses that stain the most, but tinted lip balms are also very popular; Rom&nd and Peripera have good ones

Rom&nd

Glasting Water Tint
  • glossy lip tint
  • no color transfer; gloss & tint somehow separate so gloss always stays on top
  • #05 ROSE SPLASH is a pretty dark red

Rom&nd

Juicy Lasting Tint
  • glossy lip tint
  • a LOT of colors in other collectons; Bare Juicy, Ripe Fruits, Milk Grocery, etc.
  • #25 BARE GRAPE is popular bc Wonyoung uses it

Peripera

Ink Mood Glowy Tint
  • glossy lip tint
  • long lasting tint; almost gets darker over time
  • #03 ROSE IN MIND is a pretty rose color

Peripera

Glasting Water Tint
  • satin/velvet lip tint
  • known for bright colors & moisture despite not glossy
  • #02 CELEB DEEP ROSE is a bright red with orangey undertones
